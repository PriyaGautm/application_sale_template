  • School also offers Cambridge International curriculum (CAIE) from Cambridge Early Years to AS and A levels-Cambridge Advanced program (XI & XII).
  • Please check the transport route map available on the website before applying, in case of availing transport facility. Routes and stops are subject to changes.
  • Please check the age criterion before applying.
    • Early Years EY-1 (Pre-Nursery) Child should be 3+ years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
    Early Years EY-2 (Nursery / LKG / PP-I) Child should be 4 + years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
    Early Years EY-3 (Prep / UKG / PP-II) Child should be 5 + years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
    Grade I Child should be 6 + years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
  • Note: The age norms mentioned above are followed by the school. However, if there is an order to follow the age criteria in accordance with the NEP norms, the school will have to abide by it and the candidate will be placed in the class according to the NEP norms
