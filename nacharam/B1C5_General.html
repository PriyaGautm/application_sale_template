Teaching and learning

  • Our approach helps learners and teachers to become confident, responsible, reflective, innovative and engaged. Our well experienced and internationally certified mentors actively engage students in their learning and get them university ready whether in India or aboard.

Assessment

  • Cambridge assessments prepare the learners for both National and International examinations.
  • The Primary Checkpoint at Grade 5 and Lower Secondary Checkpoint at 8 are global exams conducted in English, Math and Science. Thus, giving them a head start and preparing them for competitive exams.
  • IGCSE (GR10) & A Level (Grade 12) examinations assess the areas we know are most valuable in preparing students for the future – like deep knowledge, conceptual understanding and higher-order thinking skills. These skills get our learners ready for the mainstream competitive exams conducted in India and aboard. Hence get them ready for a world of opportunities within India and aboard.

National and International recognition

  • Cambridge programmes are recognised and valued by universities all around the world – including in India, US, the UK, Australia, Canada, Germany and beyond. Deemed universities in India like BITS Pilani, Shiv Nadar, Ashoka, SRM and many more recognise Cambridge Qualifications. All UK universities and over 800 universities in the US accept Cambridge International AS & A Levels.
  • Students can be confident that their qualifications will be valued throughout their education and career, both in their home country and internationally.
  • ICE and AICE Diploma gives the Cambridge Learners an additional advantage, wherein it given international recognition for students studying a broad spectrum of subjects.

Global community
