• Admission is subject to the availability of seats and eligibility of the candidate

  • Residential school is offered for Boys of grades VI-XII. Local guardian is mandatory.
  • Please check the age criterion before applying.
    • Early Years EY-1 (Pre-Nursery) Child should be 3+ years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
    Early Years EY-2 (Nursery / LKG / PP-I) Child should be 4+ years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
    Early Years EY-3 (Prep / UKG / PP-II) Child should be 5+ years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
    Grade I Child should be 6+ years of age as on 30th September of the year seeking for admission
  • Note: The age norms mentioned above are followed by the school. However, if there is an order to follow the age criteria in accordance with the NEP norms, the school will have to abide by it and the candidate will be placed in the grade according to the NEP norms
